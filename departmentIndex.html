University of Miami School of Architecture

    Program Description

      The University of Miami Architecture Program is a 5-year accredited degree program that allows students to directly enter the workforce upon graduation.
      The Mission of the U-SoA is to prepare students for professional leadership, to advance student knowledge of technology, and to deploy knowledge into professional engagement.
